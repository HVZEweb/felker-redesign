About Felker Brothers

Over a century of stainless steel piping expertise — from standard products to custom fabricated systems.

Our History

Since 1903

1903 — Founded

Felker Brothers Corporation established as a family-owned manufacturer serving industrial and process industries with stainless steel piping solutions.

Two divisions, one standard

Felker Piping Products and Felker Fabrication operate as specialized divisions — standard catalog items and custom engineered systems under one quality system.

ISO 9001 quality

Documented quality management with material traceability supports regulated industries including food, dairy, pharmaceutical and chemical processing.

Today

Manufacturing in Wisconsin and Kentucky with domestic distribution and international export — supporting customers who depend on corrosion-resistant process piping.
