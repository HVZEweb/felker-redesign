About Felker Brothers Corporation

Family-owned manufacturer of welded stainless steel pipe, tube, fittings and fabricated piping systems — serving process industries since 1903.

Our History

1903 — Founded

Established to serve industrial and sanitary process markets with corrosion-resistant piping solutions.

Two divisions

Felker Piping Products and Felker Fabrication — standard catalog and custom engineered systems under one ISO 9001 quality system.

Today

Manufacturing in Marshfield, Wisconsin and Mayfield, Kentucky with domestic distribution and international export.

Certifications

ISO 9001 quality management · ASTM / ASME material standards · 3A sanitary where applicable · Material traceability and MTR packages on request.

Mission

To be the complete combination in stainless steel piping products — delivering quality, documentation and service that process industries depend on.
